Dental medicine · a new generation

Precision, trust and an individual approach

Dr. Ivan Kanchev is a second-generation dental physician. He treats and prevents diseases of the teeth and periodontal tissues under magnification, with a focus on root canal therapy and the functional-aesthetic rehabilitation of the dentition.

Education & qualifications

  • Graduated from the Faculty of Dental Medicine, Medical University of Plovdiv (2016)
  • Master's in Health Management (2023)
  • Specialisation in “Prosthodontics and New Technology”, University of Siena, Italy (2024)
  • Member of the Bulgarian Dental Association (BZS)
  • Advanced training in microscopic endodontics and cosmetic dentistry
