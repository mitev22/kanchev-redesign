Dr. Ivan Kanchev — six design directions

Six completely different design languages for the practice's site, each a small four-page site (Home · Services · About · Contact). The content is identical and real across all six — services, reviews (5.0 from 356, SuperDoc), credentials, NHIF, contacts — so you're comparing pure design, not different copy.

Before you browse:
  • The content is extracted from the current site (clinics/kanchev-dental). Real photos of the practice are used where the direction calls for photography.
  • The address, 70 Maria Luiza Blvd, is temporary (the permanent office is under renovation) — it's flagged in the code with PLACEHOLDER comments, along with the photo slot for Dr. Kanchev and the map.
  • The forms are demonstrations — they don't send requests. Phase 2 wires them to Web3Forms just like the live site.
  • The current site is light teal glassmorphism, and the repo also holds three older dark drafts — these six directions deliberately repeat none of them.

How to pick a winner: open a direction, look through its four pages (and on mobile — shrink the window), then come back here and compare the next one. When you choose, reply in the session with the folder name, e.g. port 02-warm-family into the real site — the chosen system is ported into the real stack and can be rolled out across the other sites.
