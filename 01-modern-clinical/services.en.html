02 — Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do you work with the national health insurance (NHIF)?

Yes. Dr. Kanchev’s practice works with the NHIF (НЗОК). We’ll explain the coverage for your specific procedure at the examination.

How much does treatment cost, and when do I pay?

Prices are set after a thorough examination and a personalised treatment plan. You receive a clear quote before we begin — with no hidden conditions.

What does the first visit include?

A thorough examination, an X-ray if necessary, and a personalised treatment plan.

Does treatment hurt?

We aim for treatment that is as gentle and painless as possible. Working under magnification allows precision, and local anaesthesia is used when needed.

What languages do you see patients in? Do you see children?

Consultations are in Bulgarian and English. The practice also sees children.

How do I book, and what should I do in an emergency?

Book through the online form or by phone at 0896 666 828. In an emergency, call us — we’ll find the earliest available appointment.
