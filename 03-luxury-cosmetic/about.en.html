About

Precision, trust and an individual approach

Dr. Ivan Kanchev is a second-generation dental physician.

He treats and prevents diseases of the teeth and periodontal tissues under magnification, with a focus on root canal therapy and the functional-aesthetic rehabilitation of the dentition.
